## Overview

Greenvy is a sustainable product rating platform focused on responsible consumption and aligned with SDG 12. It combines a blog management workflow, AI-assisted moderation, and an education hub to help users publish, review, and learn from sustainability content.

## Key Features

- **Blog approval workflow** with pending submissions reviewed by admins before publication
- **Google Gemini API moderation** to detect inappropriate, toxic, or off-topic sustainability content
- **AI-driven Education Hub** that validates SDG-12 relevance and generates summaries, quizzes, glossary terms, actionable tips, and learning guides
- **Secure REST APIs** with authentication, role-based access control, pagination, and like/unlike engagement
- **Service-based architecture** using Express controllers, middleware, and Mongoose models for scalability

## Technologies Used

- **Node.js**
- **Express.js**
- **React**
- **MongoDB**
- **Google Gemini API**
- **Jest**
- **Supertest**
